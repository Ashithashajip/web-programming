SNGCE

Sree Narayana Gurukulam College of Engineering widely known as SNGCE is a pioneer, self-financing educational institution established in the year 2002 to impart engineering and management education with the -state - of- art amenities to the deserving candidates. It is well managed by Sree Narayana Gurukulam Charitable Trust, a recognized charitable society comprising of eminent personalities from diverse spheres of activity across the world and flourishes under the wings of SNDP Kunnathunadu Union. SNGCE is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Techno logical University (KTU). It is also an approved Research Centre of Kerala University of Fisheries and Ocean Studies (KUFOS).


The College is committed to serving the economically and socially marginalized sections of society. Inspired by the principles of Sree Narayana Guru - "Unify and strengthen, Edify and Enlighten", the institution is striving hard to achieve excellence by imparting professional education, front- line industrial experience and knowledge to the youth, the impending citizens of the nation.


The sincere efforts of all stakeholders in creating the up-to-date infrastructure, dedicated members of faculty, disciplined campus, opportunities for co-curricular and extra-curricular activities, systematically developed phases and procedures highlighting the personality development of each and every student - all have earned a quick reputation to the institution nationwide. SNGCE is accredited by International Accreditation Organization (IAO) for its high standard and systematic accumulation and dissemination of knowledge.
